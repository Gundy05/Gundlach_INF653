The Two Personas

Emma Richardson

Demographic Information: 32 years old, lives in a suburban area, married with one child

Job Title: Physical Therapist

Main Focus of Activity: Helping patients recover from injuries and maintain physical health through therapy

Goals: Improve accessibility to chiropractic services, ensure clients receive holistic treatment, and stay updated on new therapy techniques

Environment: Works in a local rehabilitation clinic, often collaborates with chiropractors for patient recovery

Technical/Product Domain Expertise: Knowledgeable in sports rehabilitation, massage therapy, and mobility treatments

Quote: "The best recovery isn't just about healing the body—it's about empowering the person to regain their strength and confidence."

Marcus Lee

Demographic Information: 45 years old, lives in an urban setting, passionate about fitness and wellness

Job Title: Personal Trainer

Main Focus of Activity: Guides clients through fitness plans, strength training, and injury prevention techniques

Goals: Help clients improve mobility, reduce pain, and optimize athletic performance

Environment: Works in a high-end fitness center, frequently refers clients to chiropractors for joint and muscle care

Technical/Product Domain Expertise: Specialized in exercise physiology, post-injury rehabilitation, and body mechanics

Quote: "A strong body starts with a healthy foundation—movement is medicine."
