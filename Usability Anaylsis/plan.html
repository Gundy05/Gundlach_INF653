Scenarios

Emma Richardson - Scenario

Emma is working with a high school athlete who has been struggling with knee pain post-recovery. She wants to explore chiropractic care as an additional treatment option to complement physical therapy. While searching for local clinics, she finds the website of a trusted chiropractor in her community.

She begins navigating the site, looking for information on adjustments and acupuncture. Wanting to learn more about the clinic’s philosophy, she heads to the "About" page. After reading through the content, she decides to book an appointment for her client. She clicks "Book Now" and completes the scheduling process, feeling hopeful that the combination of therapy and chiropractic care will lead to better outcomes.

Marcus Lee - Scenario

Marcus has been training a client who experiences chronic back pain, affecting their performance in the gym. He wants to help them find a long-term solution beyond exercise modifications. Knowing that chiropractic adjustments might help, he searches for a reputable clinic and lands on the website of a local chiropractor.

Marcus browses the services, paying close attention to treatments that align with his client's needs. He reads about cupping and adjustments, then moves to the contact page to ask a few questions. Feeling confident in the clinic’s offerings, he recommends his client schedule an appointment. Hoping for a seamless process, Marcus guides them through booking online and looks forward to seeing the impact of chiropractic care on his client’s progress.
